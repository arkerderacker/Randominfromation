1.𝕋𝕣𝕚𝕘𝕠𝕞𝕖𝕥𝕣𝕪

hyp = hypotenuse which is the longest side of the triangle

adj = adjacent which is always near the ø

opp = opposite which is the last side of the triangle

To find the angle or side, use the trigometric formula

tan(tangruine) = opp ÷ adj

cos(cosine) = adj ÷ hyp

sin(sine) = opp ÷ hyp

which can also be remebered by "toa cah soh"

T(tan)O(opp)A(adj)

C(cos)A(adj)H(hyp)

S(sin)O(opp)H(hyp)
